ABOUT TODDLER TOWN

TODDLER TOWN Preschool was established in the year 2010 with a vision to nurture young minds. TODDLER KIDS focuses on the core domains of early childhood development namely creative skills, language skills, numerical skills, cognitive skills, kinesthetic skills, world around me and personal-social-emotional skills.
